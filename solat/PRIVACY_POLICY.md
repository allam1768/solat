# Privacy Policy for Solat App

**Last Updated:** July 24, 2026

Thank you for choosing **Solat App** ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our application.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclosure, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application **Solat App**.

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### A. Location Data
- **What We Collect:** Approximate (coarse) and precise (fine) location data (GPS coordinates).
- **Why We Collect It:** Location data is used strictly to calculate accurate local daily prayer times (Subuh, Dzuhur, Ashar, Maghrib, Isya) and to determine the Qibla direction for your current location.
- **How It Is Handled:** All location calculations are performed locally on your device using offline algorithms (`adhan` library). Your location data is **never** uploaded to our servers, sold, or shared with any third parties.

### B. Device Information & Permissions
- **Notifications Permission:** Used solely to trigger timely alerts for prayer times and Friday prayer preparation.
- **Exact Alarms (`SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM`):** Used to schedule exact alarm triggers for timely prayer reminders and full-screen overlay notifications.
- **Overlay Window Permission (`SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW`):** Used to display full-screen reminder overlays on top of system applications when prayer time approaches.

---

## 2. Data Storage and Security
- All user preferences (such as selected theme, language, and notification toggles) are stored locally on your device using encrypted `GetStorage`.
- We do not maintain any external databases collecting personal user data.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services
Our application does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.

---

## 4. Children's Privacy
Our application is safe for users of all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
